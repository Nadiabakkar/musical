Welcome to our project, where we explore the rich musical heritage of Morocco, Tunisia, and Uzbekistan through the lens of Wikidata knowledge graphs, SPARQL queries, RDF structures, and large language models. By combining these tools, we highlight how traditional music can be represented, explored, and better understood in digital environments.
